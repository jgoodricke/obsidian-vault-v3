# Modding Philosophy
- Character:
	- I am a senior engineer who worked on the vaults, who has high intelligence but almost no experience fighting.
- Type of Game
	- Action RPG
	- Survival Game 
	- City Manager
	- Factory Builder 
- Core Goals
	- Make the core gameplay more challenging.
	- Make overworld more interesting to travel through.
	- Make wilderness dangerous, and settlements safe.
	- Improve Settlement Management
	- Improve Manufacturing
	- Improve VR Experience
- Other Goals
	- Fix bugs, especially VR bugs
	- Improve performance.
	- Minimise menu usage
	- Minimise HUD, communicate to player through other means.
	- Integrate DLC into the base game?
	- Improve visual clarity.
	- Make nights and interiors darker to enhance tension.
